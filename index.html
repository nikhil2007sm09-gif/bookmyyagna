Panchang: Your Daily Cosmic Guide 🌞🌙

Dive into the ancient wisdom of Vedic timekeeping. Panchang aligns your actions with celestial rhythms for maximum positivity.

Tithi (तिथि)

The lunar date that signifies spiritual and ritual timing. Tithi influences emotions, energies, and auspiciousness.

Nakshatra (नक्षत्र)

The star constellation the Moon resides in today. It shapes the day's mood and rituals, guiding favorable actions.

Yoga (योग)

A combination of the Sun and Moon's positions, determining auspicious timings for spiritual endeavors.

Karana (करण)

Half of a Tithi that sets the ground for daily karma. Important for choosing right Muhurat (auspicious moment).

ॐ

Vaar (वार)

The weekday, ruled by a planet. Every Vaar carries its own spiritual and astrological essence.

Sunrise & Sunset

Timings of sunrise and sunset mark transitions of the day and are critical for Sandhya Vandana and Pujas.
